Importance of Cleanliness

Cleanliness is not a work which should do forcefully. It is a good habit and healthy way of our healthy life. All type of cleanliness is very necessary for our good health whether it is personal cleanliness, surrounding cleanliness, environment cleanliness, pet animal cleanliness or work place cleanliness (like school, college, office, etc). We all should be highly aware about how to maintain cleanliness in our daily life.

Factors Effecting The Environment:

  2. Vehicles
  3. Rapid Urbanisation and Industrialisation
  4. Population Overgrowth
